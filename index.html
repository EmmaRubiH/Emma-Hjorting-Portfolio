Who am I ?

And how did I start with programming

My name is Emma Hjorting and I am a newly trained software developer.


When I was little I wanted to be an artist. And I did that for quite a few years abroad. When I worked abroad, I put a lot into photography, making movies, editing. Yes posted all this on a Blog at the time. Designed my website many times back and forth. Didn't really understand then that I loved tinkering with codes.


When a few years later I worked at (my current job) at the preschool. I took on all the extra duties as IT manager in the department. Edited, solved problems in our systems and taught this to my colleagues. Realized then that something was missing. Needed an outlet for my creative, curious and problem-solving side.

My cousin introduced me to programming, and I thought this might be for me. The training is over and I'm hooked.
